Alternate Radio Listener (UKR)

This website hosts archive.is links to audio recordings of Russian military communications recorded on frequency 1525. As of now, these recording have not been translated. The recordings are available for free on archive.is. The radio tuner is maintained by the Electronic Warfare Institute of the University of Twente.
